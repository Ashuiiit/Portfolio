About


Greetings and welcome to my world of curiosity and creativity! I am Ashutosh, a relentless explorer currently on an exhilarating journey, pursuing a BTech degree in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics at the prestigious IIIT Delhi.


From the awe-inspiring world of algorithms to the captivating realm of applied mathematics, I find myself irresistibly drawn to the wonders that technology and numbers have to offer. The thrill of uncovering solutions to complex problems and unraveling the secrets of the digital universe fuels my pursuit of knowledge.


Beyond code, I am fascinated by the art of UI/UX design. Guided by empathy and human-centric principles, I strive to create delightful and intuitive user experiences. Crafting visually stunning interfaces, I endeavor to merge the realms of creativity and functionality seamlessly.


Beyond academia, life blossoms with hues of creativity and vitality. A voracious reader and a passionate prose writer, I weave words into captivating stories that breathe life into imagination. When I'm not lost in the world of words, you might find me gracefully gliding in the pool, smashing shuttlecocks on the badminton court, painting vivid pictures on the canvas, or creating rhythmic symphonies with the tabla and dholak.


If you wish to connect, collaborate, or simply have a delightful conversation, don't hesitate to reach out!
